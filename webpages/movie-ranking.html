Aquarium Baseball

Aquarium Baseball

One of my first photoshops done in class.. the assignment was to create a collage using a given word. My word was Underwater. It started out as a simple underwater baseball scene and i thought what if it was actually inside an aquarium in a doctors office. Of course there then needed to be a child pressed against the glass to see what was going on.
